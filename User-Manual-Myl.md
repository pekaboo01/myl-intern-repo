1. What is a “User Manual for Me”, and why do teams use them?

A “User Manual for Me” is a personal guide that explains how you work, communicate, and learn best. Teams use them to understand each member’s preferences, reduce misunderstandings, and improve collaboration.

2. What categories are helpful to include?

-Communication style
-Work habits and productivity
-Feedback preferences
-Collaboration style
-Quirks and personal strengths

3. How can writing a personal user manual improve teamwork and reduce misunderstandings?

-It makes expectations clear.
-Helps teammates know how to communicate effectively with you.
-Prevents frustration by showing how you like to receive instructions and feedback.
-Supports smoother collaboration and faster learning.
