1. When do you prefer using AI vs. searching Google?

I use AI when I don’t understand something or need a quick explanation. It helps me learn and gives ideas on how to solve a problem.

I use Google when I have a specific error or need official documentation. It’s better for finding exact answers and real examples.

2. How do you decide when to ask a colleague instead?

I ask a colleague when the problem is related to our project or something I can’t find online. I also ask when I’ve been stuck for about 20–30 minutes or when I need a quick answer.

3. What challenges do developers face when troubleshooting alone?

Developers can get stuck and waste time trying the same things. It can be hard to understand errors or know what’s wrong. Also, working alone means fewer ideas, so it’s harder to find better solutions.
